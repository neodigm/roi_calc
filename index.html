What Is Cost per Lead (CPL) in Marketing?

Cost per lead, or CPL, is the amount of money that is spent to acquire one new lead from a marketing campaign. CPL helps sales teams to measure the cost-effectiveness of their campaigns, and it's the number that most sales development representative (SDR) quotas are set to. This is the outcome virtually all sales development managers focus on.

When you track this metric, you can guide your strategy in a way to maximize your return on everything you do.
